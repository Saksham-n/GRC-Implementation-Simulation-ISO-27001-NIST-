# Data Retention Schedule
**Objective:** To comply with the "Storage Limitation" principle of Privacy Laws.
**Policy:** Data is retained only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected.

| Data Category | Retention Period | Justification / Legal Basis | Action After Expiry |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **User Access Logs** | 1 Year | Mandated by **CERT-In** (Cyber Security Directions 2022). | Secure Deletion (Overwrite). |
| **Inactive User Accounts** | 2 Years | Business analytics and re-activation window. | Anonymization (PII removal). |
| **Financial/Tax Records** | 8 Years | Mandated by **Income Tax Act, 1961**. | Archive to Cold Storage. |
| **Session Cookies** | Session End | Temporary operational requirement. | Auto-expire. |
| **Customer Support Chats** | 6 Months | Quality assurance and dispute resolution. | Deletion. |

---
**Review Cycle:** This schedule is reviewed annually by the Legal and Infosec teams.
