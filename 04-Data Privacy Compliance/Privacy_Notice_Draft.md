# Data Privacy Notice (Draft)
**Region:** India
**Legislation:** Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023

## 1. Introduction
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This notice describes how we collect, process, and safeguard your information as a **Data Fiduciary**.

## 2. What Data We Collect
We adhere to the principle of **Data Minimization**. We only collect:
* **Identity Data:** Name, Phone Number (for account verification).
* **Delivery Data:** Physical Address (for order fulfillment).
* **Transactional Data:** Payment history (masked).

## 3. Purpose of Processing (Purpose Limitation)
Your data is processed strictly for:
1.  Processing and delivering your orders.
2.  Fraud detection and security.
3.  Legal compliance with tax authorities.

*We do not sell your data to third-party advertisers without your explicit consent.*

## 4. Consent Manager
* **Opt-In:** Consent is obtained via a clear, affirmative action (checkbox) at signup.
* **Withdrawal:** You may withdraw consent at any time via the "Settings > Privacy" menu.

## 5. Grievance Redressal
For any privacy concerns, contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) at:
* Email: privacy-office@example.com
* Response Time: Within 72 hours.
